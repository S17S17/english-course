Exercise 2: I've got...

In English, we use "I've got" to talk about what we have.

Examples:

  • I've got brown hair and brown eyes.
  • I've got a green notebook.
  • She's got black hair.
  • He's got blue eyes.

Fill in the blanks with the correct form:

1. I __________ a red pencil case.

2. She __________ blonde hair.

3. They __________ brown eyes.
